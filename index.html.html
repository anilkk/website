Ink Tank Tattoo- Mobile App

Ink Tank Tattoo was a 3 months project created for Career Foundry UX Design course. Ink Tank Tattoo is a mobile application for both artists and tattoo lovers meant to revolutionize the tattoo world, by creating a community where the tattoo customers can find inspirational designs, new artists and book appointments in their local area while the artists are encouraged to share their portofolio and become known all around the world.
